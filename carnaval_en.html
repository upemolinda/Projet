The Olinda Carnival


The Olinda Carnival as part of the Recife-Olinda Carnival
takes place in the historic centre of Olinda.
It is recognized worldwide by the Olinda dolls' parades,
dolls over two meters tall and colorful that parade with the fetards.

It brings together more than a million people during the event, with the
participation of about 500 carnival groups.
